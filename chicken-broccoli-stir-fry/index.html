    Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry

    Source: Skinnytaste

    This easy Chicken and Broccoli recipe is made with strips of chicken breast and broccoli in a ginger-garlic stir-fry sauce.

    Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Total Time: 30 minutes Servings: 4

    Ingredients

    • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce (or gluten-free option)
    • 1/2 cup chicken broth
    • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
    • 2 tablespoons mirin
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
    • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
    • 1 tablespoon canola oil (divided)
    • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
    • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
    • 1 pound chicken breast (sliced very thinly)
    • 2 cups broccoli florets
    • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

    Instructions

    1. Combine soy sauce, broth, cornstarch, mirin, sugar, sesame oil, and white pepper in a measuring cup; whisk until dissolved.

    2. Heat half the canola oil in a large skillet over medium-high; cook half the chicken about 2-3 minutes per side until cooked through.

    3. Repeat with remaining chicken and oil.

    4. Remove chicken to a plate.

    5. Add remaining oil, garlic, and ginger; cook 30-45 seconds until fragrant.

    6. Add sauce mixture and whisk well.

    7. Add broccoli; simmer 2-3 minutes until sauce thickens.

    8. Return chicken to skillet; stir to coat.

    9. Garnish with sesame seeds if desired.

    Nutrition

    Per serving: 249 calories, 27g protein, 14.6g carbs, 9g fat
