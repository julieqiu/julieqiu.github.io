    Mom's Sauce

    Ingredients

    • Cilantro (chopped finely)
    • Garlic (chopped finely)
    • Scallions (chopped finely)
    • Oil (any kind)
    • Sichuan Chili Powder
    • Soy Sauce
    • Chinese Vinegar

    Instructions

    1. Put garlic and chili powder in a heat-safe bowl.

    2. Heat up oil in a small pan until sizzling, then pour over the garlic and chili powder.

    3. Wait 1 minute, then mix with cilantro, scallions, vinegar and soy sauce.
