    Steamed Spicy Chinese Eggplant

    Ingredients

    • 4 Chinese Eggplant
    • White Vinegar
    • Garlic
    • Scallions
    • Oil
    • Mom’s Sauce

    Equipment

    • Steamer

    Instructions

    1. Boil water and prepare your steamer.

    2. Cut each eggplant in half, then into 4 equal sections (yielding 8 strips per eggplant).

    3. (Optional step): Fill a large container with about 2 quarts of water and 1/4 cup white vinegar. Soak the eggplant in the vinegar water for 3 minutes. Then remove the eggplant and squeeze as much liquid out as possible. Then put the eggplant on the steamer.

    4. Cover and steam the eggplant for 8-10 minutes.

    5. Remove the eggplant from the steamer and pour out any excess liquid.

    6. Pour Mom’s Sauce over the eggplant.

    7. Put minced garlic + scallions in a bowl. Heat up oil in a saucepan, then pour over garlic + scallions. While hot, pour entire mixture over the eggplant. Alternatively, you can top the eggplant with minced garlic + scallions and pour the oil over that section.
