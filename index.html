Hi! I'm Julie, @jqiu25.
I’m a software engineer on the Go team at Google in NYC.
I joined Google in October 2018 and work on the Go Open Source team, which is responsible for maintaining the Go standard library. I am also currently leading the project to build a site for developers to find dependable Go packages and modules, which I’ve spoken about a few conferences this year. 
Prior to joining Google, I led the Catalog team at Spring, an e-commerce marketplace which has since been acquired by ShopRunner.
I attended the Recurse Center in July 2015 and was part of the Summer 2 batch.
