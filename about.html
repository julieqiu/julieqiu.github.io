Hi! I'm Julie, @jqiu25 .
I’m a software engineer on the Go programming language team at Google in NYC.

Prior to joining Google, I was the TLM for the Catalog team at Spring, an e-commerce marketplace which has since been acquired by ShopRunner.
I also joined the Recurse Center community in July 2015, as a recurser in the Summer 2 batch.
